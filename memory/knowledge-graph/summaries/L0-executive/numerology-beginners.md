## What is Numerology?

Numerology is the study of numbers and their mystical significance in human life. It operates on the principle that numbers carry vibrational frequencies that influence personality, destiny, and life events.

---

## Core Systems Covered

### Pythagorean System
- Most commonly used Western system
- Letters A-Z mapped to numbers 1-9
- Based on Pythagoras's mathematical principles

### Chaldean System
- Ancient Babylonian system
- Different letter-to-number mappings
- Emphasizes sound vibrations over sequence

---

## Key Numbers Explained

### Life Path Number
- Derived from birth date
- Represents life's purpose and journey
- Most important number in numerology

### Destiny/Expression Number
- Derived from full birth name
- Represents talents, abilities, and potential

### Soul Urge Number
- Derived from vowels in name
- Represents inner desires and motivations

### Personality Number
- Derived from consonants in name
- Represents outward personality and first impressions

### Birthday Number
- Day of birth (1-31)
- Represents specific talents and challenges

---

## Special Numbers

### Master Numbers (11, 22, 33)
- Higher vibrational frequencies
- Not reduced to single digits
- Indicate special spiritual missions

### Karmic Debt Numbers (13, 14, 16, 19)
- Indicate lessons from past lives
- Challenges to overcome in this lifetime
