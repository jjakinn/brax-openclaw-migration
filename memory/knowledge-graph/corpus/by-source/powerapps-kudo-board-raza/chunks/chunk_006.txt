CHUNK 6: BOARD LOCKING FEATURE
Source: powerapps-kudo-board-raza | Timestamp: 03:15 - 04:00

Raza, who created the board, has the option to lock the board.

---

Once the creator of the board locks the board, no more posts can be added to the board.

---

Notice for Sarah, the board for James shows as locked.

---

Sarah can select and see all the details but cannot add more posts to the board.

---

For any other boards that are open, Sarah can post to that board.

---

KEY POINTS:
- Board locking: Creator can lock board
- Effect of locking:
  - No new posts allowed
  - Existing posts remain visible
  - Shows "locked" status
- Permissions:
  - Locked: View only for all users
  - Open: Posting allowed
- Creator control: Only board creator can lock
