CHUNK 10: CREATING GENERATIVE PAGES
Source: powerapps-kudo-board-raza | Timestamp: 06:15 - 06:45

I've done a full series of videos on how to build generative pages. Do check that out.

---

To create them, simply go to new, describe a page.

---

Add references to your tables. Provide images as inspiration for the app agent. Describe what you need. Hit generate and it will start framing the gen page.

---

KEY POINTS:
- Generative pages workflow:
  1. New → Describe a page
  2. Add table references
  3. Provide inspiration images (optional)
  4. Describe requirements
  5. Generate
- Output: React page created by AI
- Resources: Full video series available
