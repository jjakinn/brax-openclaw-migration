CHUNK 8: DATA MODEL - BOARDS AND POSTS TABLES
Source: powerapps-kudo-board-raza | Timestamp: 04:45 - 05:30

Two simple tables:

---

Boards: Here I store:
- The title
- The recipient (lookup to user table)
- Column to track whether board is locked or not

---

The second table is Posts. This includes:
- The title of the post
- The body (rich text)
- Board (lookup column to boards table) — to which board does this post belong
- Column for image/gif (user uploads or selects)

---

KEY POINTS:
- Table structure:
  1. Boards table:
     - Title
     - Recipient (lookup to User)
     - Locked status
  2. Posts table:
     - Title
     - Body (rich text)
     - Board (lookup to Boards)
     - Image/GIF column
- Relationship: Boards → Posts (one-to-many)
