CHUNK 2: APP BUILDER AGENT IN M365 COPILOT
Source: powerapps-tools-ecosystem-raza | Timestamp: 01:00 - 03:00

The app builder agent in M365 Copilot is meant for personal and team productivity based scenarios.

For enterprise scale scenarios, there are options from low code to vibe code to pro code.

---

Now let's go through each of these experiences one by one. Let's start with app builder specific to M365 Copilot.

The back end that it utilizes is SharePoint. No connectors, no formulas, no Power App Studio. You simply describe your app needs in natural language.

---

In M365 Copilot, in the Frontier program, we have the new app builder agent.

Here we can simply describe in everyday words what kind of an application we would like to build. Since it's a part of M365 copilot, the creator of this application and the users to whom you share this app with will require an M365 Copilot license.

---

My description: "Build me an app where people can submit ideas. It should capture the title, description, impact, and let others vote on the ideas."

I'll go ahead and begin the process of creating this app.

---

KEY POINTS:
- App Builder Agent: Personal and team productivity scenarios
- Location: M365 Copilot (Frontier program)
- Backend: SharePoint
- Requirements: M365 Copilot license for creator AND users
- Approach: Natural language description → App generated
- No: Connectors, formulas, Power Apps Studio needed
