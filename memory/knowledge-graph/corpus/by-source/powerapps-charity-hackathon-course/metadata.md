## Overview

End-to-end Power Apps and Dataverse solution build for Potter's House charity in Guatemala. Multiple expert instructors collaborate on a real-world project helping children with education and nutritional needs.

## Charity Context: Potter's House

**Mission:** Help Guatemalan children with education and nutritional needs
**Context:** Average Guatemalan child receives only 2.8 years of schooling
**Approach:** Holistic development programs (education + nutrition)
**Duration:** 38+ years of service

### Real-World Impact Story: Alexis
- Lives in fenero Chima, Guatemala
- Wakes at 6:00 AM for school
- Participates in Potter's House programs after school
- Receives nutritious meals for physical/cognitive development
- Has improved reading fluency through education programs
- Family and Potter's House working together for better future
