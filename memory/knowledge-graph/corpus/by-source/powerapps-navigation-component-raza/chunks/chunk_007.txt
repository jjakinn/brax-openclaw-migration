The items property for this gallery, my nav structure.

Now observe the moment I point to a formula or a variable within my app, my component does not get its context.

To give it the context, back to the component, there is a property called `AccessAppScope`. This we will turn on.

The moment I turn this on, now my component can start working with formulas, variables, and more that were defined within the app.
