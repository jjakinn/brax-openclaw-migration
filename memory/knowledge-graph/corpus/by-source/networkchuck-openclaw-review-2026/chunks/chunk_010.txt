- Fourth pillar: TOOLS — Bash scripts, cron jobs, heartbeats
- Philosophy: "This is YOUR machine. Here are YOUR tools."
- **Heartbeats**: Agent-initiated check-ins ("Hey, just checking on you")
- **Crons**: Scheduled tasks on the actual server cron
- Makes agent feel "alive" — proactivity vs reactivity

- Cron jobs are REAL system crontab entries
- CLI: `openclaw cron list` to view all scheduled jobs
- Agent can choose cron vs heartbeat based on task
