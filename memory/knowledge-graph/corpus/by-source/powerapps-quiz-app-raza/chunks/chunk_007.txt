Now let's explore the admin experience.

---

The quiz admin app shows a list of active quizzes.

---

For each quiz, I can define:
- The name
- Description
- The category
- The pass percentage
- The maximum number of attempts allowed

---

Once I have my quiz defined, I can go ahead and start framing my questions.
