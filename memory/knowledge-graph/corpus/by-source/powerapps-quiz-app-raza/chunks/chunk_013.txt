CHUNK 13: DATA MODEL AND RELATIONSHIPS
Source: powerapps-quiz-app-raza | Timestamp: 09:30 - 10:00

Let's explore the data workspace.

---

These are the four tables being used by the application.

---

For each quiz, there are going to be multiple questions. So there's a one-to-many relationship between these two tables.

---

A user can take a quiz multiple times. So a quiz can have multiple attempts.

---

The attempt table is where the results are stored and this includes the information of the user. That's the participant who took the quiz.

---

KEY POINTS:
- Four tables in data model:
  1. Quiz
  2. Question
  3. Attempt
  4. (User/Participant)
- Relationships:
  - Quiz → Questions: One-to-many
  - Quiz → Attempts: One-to-many
- Attempt table: Stores quiz results and user info
